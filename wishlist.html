Our Wishlist



We have been asked to post our Wish List for the farm. So here goes! Some may be big ticket items and some cast offs you have in your yard. All are appreciated.



Yard Stuff

  • Pitch forks/shovels/rakes of any kind

  • Any olive/sage/dark green exterior paint

  • Little Tykes Houses/Slides etc for play yards for baby goats.

  • Wheelbarrows of any type

  • Tractor/Riding Mower with or without deck

  • Electric chipper (small for banana leaves and greens for the composter/soldier fly larvae harvester)

  • 4 x 4 posts any length

  • Clear or smoked color corrugated plastic roofing

Misc
